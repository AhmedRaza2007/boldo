About Edgar Allan Poe

Edgar Allan Poe was an American writer, poet, editor, and literary critic, best known for his tales of mystery and the macabre. He is considered a central figure in the history of American literature.

Famous Works

The Raven

The Raven is one of Poe's most famous poems, filled with dark imagery and haunting rhythm.

The Tell-Tale Heart

The Tell-Tale Heart is a short story that reveals the paranoia and guilt of a man who commits a murder.

Poe's Legacy

Poe's work influenced countless writers, filmmakers, and artists, establishing him as one of the most enduring figures in the Gothic and horror genres.
